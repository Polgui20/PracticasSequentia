About us

Sequentia arrives to the digital world with the aim to transform the traditional model for obituary publications and communications, changing it into an easier process, intimate, with no strict schedules and no conditions. A model reachable for all of us. You decide what and how to communicate and, at the same time, you will always be informed, deciding who has access to your publications, who can send you comments and what is the best way for being present.

In Sequentia you will find a sensitive, generous, compromised and responsible community that values sharing, being present and giving support to other people in difficult times.

¿How can we help you?

The best way for being present

We all like to decide how and when to communicate. Whether you need to share something or if you want to be informed and present, in Sequentia we offer you a safe, close and intimate space in which:


Easy communication

Everyone can easily communicate a decease just using a single publication, choosing who can read your posts and which information you want to share. You set the rules.


Being present

You can decide how do you want others to accompany you, whether it’s sending their condolences, flowers, printing your comments book or the combination you choose.



Always informed

Stay always informed so you can accompany each person the best possible way and when it’s needed.





Our services

Now you can communicate, give support and be present in an intimate, easy and close way, with services in a click. What can we offer?


Flowers Shipping

Sending flowers is just a one click service. We facilitate the process so you can give all your support to your loved ones.

Digital flowers

If you want to be present, even when you are far away, we offer you a selection of digital flowers that you can send anytime from anywhere.


Being present

In Sequentia we offer a space where you can write a message with no restrictions, no conditions, at your own pace, anytime, anywhere.





Comments book

If you decide to communicate your loss, we can print all your messages received in a comments book with pictures that we will send to the address that you choose.
